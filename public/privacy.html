Privacy Policy

Aryash Health Professional Services

Summary: Minimal data collection

This website is designed for healthcare professionals, practice managers, and GP partners. We collect minimal data, limited to what you voluntarily provide through contact forms. No patient data is processed through this site.

Who this site is for

This professional services site (pro.aryash.health) is intended for:

  • GPs and GP Partners
  • GP Trainees and Registrars
  • GP Trainers and Educators
  • Practice Managers
  • Healthcare professionals interested in AI tools

If you're looking for patient-facing resources, please visit tools.aryash.health.

What we do NOT collect

  • Patient information or clinical data
  • Your IP address for identification or analytics
  • Cookies or tracking identifiers
  • Usage analytics or behavioural data

What we DO collect

We only collect information you voluntarily provide:

Contact Form

  • Name and email address
  • Your professional role (GP, Trainee, Manager, etc.)
  • Your message content

This information is processed via Formspree and used solely to respond to your enquiry. We do not add you to mailing lists without explicit consent.

Waitlist Sign-up

If you join a waitlist for AI tools, we collect your email address to notify you when tools become available. You can request removal at any time.

Practice Benchmarking Tools

The Practice Health Check and Practice Comparison tools help GP partners and managers understand their practice performance. These tools:

  • Run entirely in your browser — no data is sent to our servers
  • Use publicly available NHS Digital data (workforce, QOF, patient registration)
  • Do not store or retain any information you enter
  • Do not use cookies or tracking

Practice Health Check: You manually enter practice figures (list size, WTE, income, staff costs). All calculations happen locally in your browser. Nothing is transmitted or stored.

Practice Comparison: Searches and displays published NHS Digital data for 6,158 practices. The practice code may appear in your browser URL for bookmarking — this is public data already available from NHS Digital.

AI Demo Tools

The demonstration tools (Practice Pulse, Policy Decoder, Partners Playbook) showcase AI functionality. When using demos:

  • Do not enter real patient information
  • Do not enter sensitive practice data
  • Demo interactions may be processed by AI services (Claude, OpenAI) but are not stored or used for training

Hosting

This website is hosted on Vercel. Standard server logs may be processed as part of their infrastructure. We do not access or use these logs to identify visitors. See Vercel's Privacy Policy for details.

Third-party services

  • Formspree — processes contact form submissions
  • Google Fonts — provides typography (no tracking)

We do not use Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, or other tracking services.

Data retention

Contact form submissions and waitlist emails are retained only as long as necessary for their purpose. You can request deletion of your data at any time by emailing us.

Your rights

Under UK GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Request access to any data we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent for communications

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as our services evolve. Given our minimal data collection, significant changes are unlikely. Any updates will be reflected on this page.
